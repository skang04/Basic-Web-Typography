HARMONY

I don’t really understand the concept of how Graphic Design is “neutral”.

What does the author mean when he asserts that design is not philosophy?

I agree that racial diversity in a creative department is important for an array of different types of designs.

How is the typeface Helvetica so “refreshing” like “an ice cold water in a hot desert”?

I agree that “good Graphic Design” has to be universally understood with some common grounds.

There are some correlation between Graphic Design and “Whiteness” but I feel like they are not mutually exclusive and the metaphor is stretching it a bit too far.
